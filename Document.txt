
# KPI Definitions - Insurance Claims Analytics Dashboard

This document contains all KPI formulas used in the Insurance Claims Analytics Dashboard built using Qlik Sense.

This project uses synthetic/sample data and is created for portfolio, learning, and demonstration purposes.

---

# 1. Claims Overview Dashboard KPIs

---

## 1.1 Opening Balance Claims

Opening Balance represents the claims that were already outstanding or unclaimed at the beginning of the selected period.


Opening Balance Claims = Claims outstanding at the start of selected period



Opening Balance Amount = Claim amount of policies outstanding at the start of selected period


***

## 1.2 Booked Claims

Booked Claims are the claims booked for settlement during the selected period.


Booked Claims = Count of claims booked during selected period



Booked Claim Amount = Sum of claim amount for claims booked during selected period


***

## 1.3 Payable Claims

Payable Claims are calculated by adding Opening Balance Claims and Booked Claims.


Payable Claims = Opening Balance Claims + Booked Claims



Payable Claim Amount = Opening Balance Amount + Booked Claim Amount


***

## 1.4 Paid Claims

Paid Claims are the claims for which payment was completed during the selected period.


Paid Claims = Count of claims where claim_status = 'Paid'



Paid Claim Amount = Sum of claim_amount where claim_status = 'Paid'


***

## 1.5 Paid On or Before Due Date

This KPI identifies claims paid on or before the claim payment due date.


Paid On or Before Due Date = Count of claims where Paid Date <= Due Date



Paid On or Before Due Date Amount = Sum of claim_amount where Paid Date <= Due Date


***

## 1.6 Claims Repudiated

Claims Repudiated represents claims that were repudiated during the selected period.


Claims Repudiated = Count of claims where claim_status = 'Repudiated'



Claims Repudiated Amount = Sum of claim_amount where claim_status = 'Repudiated'


***

## 1.7 Claims Rejected

Claims Rejected represents claims that were rejected during the selected period.


Claims Rejected = Count of claims where claim_status = 'Rejected'


Claims Rejected Amount = Sum of claim_amount where claim_status = 'Rejected'


***

## 1.8 Claims Cancelled

Claims Cancelled represents claims that were cancelled during the selected period.


Claims Cancelled = Count of claims where claim_status = 'Cancelled'



Claims Cancelled Amount = Sum of claim_amount where claim_status = 'Cancelled'


***

## 1.9 Claims Intimation Cancelled

Claims Intimation Cancelled represents claims where claim intimation was cancelled.


Claims Intimation Cancelled = Count of claims where claim_status = 'Intimation Cancelled'



Claims Intimation Cancelled Amount = Sum of claim_amount where claim_status = 'Intimation Cancelled'


***

## 1.10 Outstanding More Than 12 Months

This KPI identifies claims that have remained outstanding for more than 12 months.


Outstanding More Than 12 Months = Count of claims where claim is outstanding for more than 12 months



Outstanding More Than 12 Months Amount = Sum of claim_amount for claims outstanding for more than 12 months


***

## 1.11 Outstanding Claims

Outstanding Claims are calculated after subtracting paid, rejected, repudiated, cancelled, intimation cancelled, and claims outstanding more than 12 months from payable claims.


Outstanding Claims = Payable Claims - Paid Claims - Outstanding More Than 12 Months - Claims Repudiated - Claims Rejected - Claims Intimation Cancelled - Claims Cancelled



Outstanding Claim Amount = Payable Claim Amount - Paid Claim Amount - Outstanding More Than 12 Months Amount - Repudiated Amount - Rejected Amount - Intimation Cancelled Amount - Cancelled Amount


***

## 1.12 Total Outstanding Claims

Total Outstanding Claims include both regular outstanding claims and claims outstanding for more than 12 months.


Total Outstanding Claims = Outstanding Claims + Outstanding More Than 12 Months



Total Outstanding Amount = Outstanding Claim Amount + Outstanding More Than 12 Months Amount


***

## 1.13 Outstanding Less Than 1 Month

Claims outstanding for less than one month from due date.


Outstanding Less Than 1 Month = Count of outstanding claims where outstanding age < 30 days



Outstanding Less Than 1 Month Amount = Sum of claim_amount where outstanding age < 30 days


***

## 1.14 Outstanding Greater Than 1 Month

Claims outstanding for more than one month from due date.


Outstanding Greater Than 1 Month = Count of outstanding claims where outstanding age > 30 days



Outstanding Greater Than 1 Month Amount = Sum of claim_amount where outstanding age > 30 days


***

## 1.15 Claim Settlement Ratio

Claim Settlement Ratio shows the percentage of payable claims that were paid.


Claim Settlement Ratio = (Paid Claims / Payable Claims) * 100


***

## 1.16 Paid Percentage

Paid Percentage shows the percentage of payable claims that have been paid.


Paid % = (Paid Claims / Payable Claims) * 100


***

## 1.17 Outstanding Percentage

Outstanding Percentage shows the percentage of payable claims that are still outstanding.


Outstanding % = (Total Outstanding Claims / Payable Claims) * 100


***

## 1.18 Speed Ratio

Speed Ratio measures how many booked claims were paid on or before the due date.


Speed Ratio = (Paid On or Before Due Date / Booked Claims) * 100


***

## 1.19 Super Speed Ratio

Super Speed Ratio measures how many claims were settled within 15 days from intimation.


Super Speed Ratio = (Claims Settled Within 15 Days From Intimation / Booked Claims) * 100

# 2. Monthly Trend KPIs

***

## 2.1 Monthly Claims Settled


Monthly Claims Settled = Count of paid claims grouped by month


***

## 2.2 Monthly Claims Amount Paid


Monthly Claims Amount Paid = Sum of claim_amount for paid claims grouped by month

***

## 2.3 Monthly Claims Outstanding


Monthly Claims Outstanding = Count of outstanding claims grouped by month


***

## 2.4 Monthly Claims Outstanding Amount


Monthly Claims Outstanding Amount = Sum of outstanding claim amount grouped by month


***

## 2.5 Monthly Claim Settlement Ratio


Monthly Claim Settlement Ratio = (Monthly Paid Claims / Monthly Payable Claims) * 100


***

# 3. Outstanding Claims Analysis Dashboard KPIs

***

## 3.1 Total Outstanding Claims


Total Outstanding Claims = Outstanding Claims + Outstanding More Than 12 Months


***

## 3.2 Total Outstanding Amount


Total Outstanding Amount = Outstanding Amount + Outstanding More Than 12 Months Amount


***

## 3.3 Outstanding Claims With Mobile Number


Outstanding Claims With Mobile = Count of outstanding claims where mobile_number is not null


***

## 3.4 Outstanding Claims Without Mobile Number


Outstanding Claims Without Mobile = Count of outstanding claims where mobile_number is null


***

## 3.5 Outstanding Claims With Email


Outstanding Claims With Email = Count of outstanding claims where email is not null


***

## 3.6 Outstanding Claims Without Email


Outstanding Claims Without Email = Count of outstanding claims where email is null


***

## 3.7 Outstanding Claims With NEFT


Outstanding Claims With NEFT = Count of outstanding claims where neft_status = 'Available'


***

## 3.8 Outstanding Claims Without NEFT


Outstanding Claims Without NEFT = Count of outstanding claims where neft_status = 'Not Available'


***

## 3.9 Outstanding Claims With PAN


Outstanding Claims With PAN = Count of outstanding claims where pan_number is not null


***

## 3.10 Outstanding Claims Without PAN


Outstanding Claims Without PAN = Count of outstanding claims where pan_number is null


***

## 3.11 Outstanding Without Mobile Percentage


Outstanding Without Mobile % = (Outstanding Claims Without Mobile / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

## 3.12 Outstanding Without Email Percentage


Outstanding Without Email % = (Outstanding Claims Without Email / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

## 3.13 Outstanding Without NEFT Percentage


Outstanding Without NEFT % = (Outstanding Claims Without NEFT / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

## 3.14 Outstanding Without PAN Percentage


Outstanding Without PAN % = (Outstanding Claims Without PAN / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

# 4. Reinvestment Analysis Dashboard KPIs

***

## 4.1 Claims With Reinvestment Consent


Claims With Reinvestment Consent = Count of outstanding claims where reinvestment_consent = 'Yes'


***

## 4.2 Claims Without Reinvestment Consent


Claims Without Reinvestment Consent = Count of outstanding claims where reinvestment_consent = 'No'


***

## 4.3 Reinvestment Consent Percentage


Reinvestment Consent % = (Claims With Reinvestment Consent / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

## 4.4 Total Reinvestment Amount


Total Reinvestment Amount = Sum of reinvestment_amount where reinvestment_consent = 'Yes'


***

## 4.5 Outstanding Claims Eligible for Reinvestment


Outstanding Claims Eligible for Reinvestment = Count of outstanding claims where reinvestment_eligible = 'Yes'


***

## 4.6 Reinvestment Eligibility Percentage


Reinvestment Eligibility % = (Outstanding Claims Eligible for Reinvestment / Total Outstanding Claims) * 100


***

## 4.7 Average Reinvestment Amount


Average Reinvestment Amount = Total Reinvestment Amount / Claims With Reinvestment Consent


***

# 5. Due Claims Analysis Dashboard KPIs

***

## 5.1 Total Claims Due


Total Claims Due = Count of claims where due_date falls in selected period


***

## 5.2 Total Claims Amount Due


Total Claims Amount Due = Sum of claim_amount where due_date falls in selected period


***

## 5.3 Total Claims Intimated


Total Claims Intimated = Count of due claims where intimation_status = 'Intimated'


***

## 5.4 Total Claims Not Intimated


Total Claims Not Intimated = Count of due claims where intimation_status = 'Not Intimated'


***

## 5.5 Claims Intimation Percentage


Claims Intimation % = (Total Claims Intimated / Total Claims Due) * 100


***

## 5.6 Claims Not Intimated Percentage


Claims Not Intimated % = (Total Claims Not Intimated / Total Claims Due) * 100


***

## 5.7 Due Claims Amount Intimated


Due Claims Amount Intimated = Sum of claim_amount where intimation_status = 'Intimated'


***

## 5.8 Due Claims Amount Not Intimated


Due Claims Amount Not Intimated = Sum of claim_amount where intimation_status = 'Not Intimated'


***

# 6. Current Year, Last Year and YoY KPIs

***

## 6.1 Current Year Number


CY Number = Count of selected metric for current financial year


***

## 6.2 Last Year Number


LY Number = Count of selected metric for same period in previous financial year


***

## 6.3 Current Year Amount


CY Amount = Sum of selected metric amount for current financial year


***

## 6.4 Last Year Amount


LY Amount = Sum of selected metric amount for same period in previous financial year


***

## 6.5 CY vs LY Difference


CY - LY = Current Year Value - Last Year Value


***

## 6.6 CY Percentage vs LY Percentage Difference


CY % - LY % = Current Year Percentage - Last Year Percentage


***

## 6.7 YoY Growth Percentage


YoY Growth % = ((Current Year Value - Last Year Value) / Last Year Value) * 100


***

## 6.8 YoY Amount Growth Percentage


YoY Amount Growth % = ((Current Year Amount - Last Year Amount) / Last Year Amount) * 100


***

## 6.9 YoY Count Growth Percentage


YoY Count Growth % = ((Current Year Count - Last Year Count) / Last Year Count) * 100


***

# 9. Recommended Formatting Rules

***

## 9.1 Positive Growth


If YoY Growth % >= 0, show green color with upward indicator.


***

## 9.2 Negative Growth


If YoY Growth % < 0, show red color with downward indicator.


***

## 9.3 Inverse Color Logic for Outstanding KPIs

For Outstanding Percentage, lower value is better.


If Outstanding % decreases, show green color.
If Outstanding % increases, show red color.
